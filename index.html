Bank was founded as an Islamic investment bank in 1997 by oor Financial, Pak Kuwait Investment Company, and Islamic Development Bank. It was then known as Al-Meezan Investment Bank. In 2000, Meezan Bank was listed on the Karachi Stock Exchange.
